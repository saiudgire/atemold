About Us

From its beginning in 1992, AT&E has strived to be a customer oriented company. Today the company is recognized as a quality supplier of high performance molds to the blow molding, thermoforming, and pressure forming industries.

Providing product design, mold engineering, mold manufacturing and mold maintenance services for all phases of blowmolding, thermoforming, pressure forming and roto molding. AT&E serves consumer, automotive and industrial customers throughout the continental United States.
